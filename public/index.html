About

It is always exciting to watch people come alive. From purely “physical life” demonstrations to intensive scene studies. I have many many students - all kinds of clients, actors primarily, doctors & lawyers, financial owners of companies, CEOs & what’s constant is that everyone has something they need to work on. No matter how skilled you are, you need to work on that new part or new demonstration, new presentation & there’s a skill to learning and memorizing while bringing life to your role and presentation.


It is my goal and my pleasure to see progress - If you don’t find progress in what you’re doing with me, i don’t like someone to continue with me - i don’t like to continue unless you see the returns, you’re doing well - i don’t want to have professional students. I need you to feel that you’re growing - I do not berate, i won’t try to intimidate you - i don’t believe in that method. i teach the psychology of acting - first and best is great but different sometimes beats even first & best.


As you'll see, I work with students of all ages, and, therefore, so will you. My students have found great success in working within diverse classes.
